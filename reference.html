Jonathan Avila





Hi, I'm Jonathan,


I'm a Ecuadorean-American from Queens who is passionate about applying design thinking and data analytics to improve people's daily lives. I recently graduated from Penn with my Bachelor's in Economics and Master's in Computer and Information Technology in December. While at school, I spent summers working in product at Redfin and Facebook and helped develop a course on statistical learning and inference for Economists.

In August, I am excited to be joining Dropbox as a New Grad Product Manager. During my time off, I plan to rest with friends and family, do some travelling and indluge in my hobbies such as hiking and reading!

I am also interested in pursuing opportunities in venture capital, data science and artificial intelligence research in the next few months: please feel free to email me!
